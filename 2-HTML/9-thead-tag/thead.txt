Thead tag: The top most of our table we have to write within <thead></thead> tag.

Tfood tag: The bottom most of our table we have to write within <tfood></tfood> tag.

Tbody tag: Remaining content of our table we have to write within <tbody></tbody> tag.

Note:- Thead, Tbody and Tfood tag are highly recommended to use inside a table for better explanation and crawler understanding purpose.
